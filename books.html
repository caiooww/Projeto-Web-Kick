The Chalk Man

Rating: 4.5/5

Review: "The Chalk Man" is a gripping thriller that keeps you on the edge of your seat from start to finish. The author, C.J. Tudor, masterfully weaves together a story of suspense, mystery, and dark secrets. The characters are well-developed and the plot is full of twists and turns that will leave you guessing until the very end. With its atmospheric setting and clever storytelling, "The Chalk Man" is a must-read for fans of psychological thrillers.

Launch date: 2018-01-09

Pages: 280

Author: C.J. Tudor

The Silent Patient

Rating: 4.5/5

Review: "The Silent Patient" is a psychological thriller that will keep you guessing until the very end. The story is told from the perspective of Theo Faber, a psychotherapist who becomes obsessed with Alicia Berenson, a famous painter who has been silent since she was accused of murdering her husband. As Theo delves deeper into Alicia's past, he uncovers dark secrets that will change everything he thought he knew. With its clever plot twists and complex characters, "The Silent Patient" is a must-read for fans of the genre.

Launch date: 2019-02-05

Pages: 336

Author: Alex Michaelides

Anne Frank - The Diary of a Young Girl

Rating: 4.5/5

Review: "The Diary of a Young Girl" is a powerful and moving story about the life of Anne Frank, a Jewish girl who was forced into hiding by the Nazis during World War II. The book is a collection of Anne's diary entries, written while she and her family were in hiding in Amsterdam. Anne's writing is honest, insightful, and full of hope, even in the face of unimaginable hardship. Her story is a reminder of the power of the human spirit and the importance of standing up against injustice.

Launch date: 1947-06-25

Pages: 283

Author: Anne Frank
